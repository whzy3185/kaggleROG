## One-parameter direct-TVT shrinkage

A direct TVT slope is fit on the last 30 visible rows, clipped to `[-0.1, 0.1]`, then multiplied by one global shrinkage coefficient. The coefficient is learned analytically on training wells.

- Full-fit coefficient: `0.0250460`
- Five-fold coefficients: `0.02816, 0.02213, 0.03304, 0.01571, 0.02636`
- Five-fold by-well OOF pooled RMSE: `15.8350`
- Full-fit pooled RMSE: `15.7623`

This is a real but small gain. It was not submitted because the daily budget is five and local validation already answers the question.

## Why naive multi-cut gating can mislead

Using cuts at 40%, 60%, and 80% of the visible prefix made a per-well trend calibration saturate at its maximum coefficient for most wells. Extending those locally preferred coefficients to the real suffix worsened pooled RMSE to `17.0369`.

The lesson is that more synthetic cuts are not automatically more honest. Cut positions must match the real prediction-start distribution and regime. Early-lateral dynamics can systematically overstate trend persistence at the supplied boundary.

### Multi-seed U-state particle tracker

Full true-start replay on all 773 wells used 300 particles, eight seeds, likelihood temperature 20, and deterministic seed base `20260717`:

| Candidate | Pooled RMSE | Macro | p95 | Worst |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Anchor | 15.9099 | 12.8125 | 29.0108 | 70.6394 |
| Particle | 14.1804 | 10.7295 | 27.2604 | 64.0802 |
| 50/50 blend | 13.2356 | 10.4683 | 24.3503 | 53.3632 |
| 65% particle blend | 13.1167 | 10.2205 | 25.1560 | 56.5684 |

The analytically optimal full-data particle weight was `0.6253` with fitted RMSE `13.1119`. More importantly, five well-group folds learned weights `0.6272, 0.6294, 0.6632, 0.5872, 0.6169`; applying each weight only to its held-out fold gave pooled RMSE `13.1544`. This shows that most of the gain survives out-of-fold blend estimation.

The blend is promising, but likelihood weights still collapse on some wells and wrong GR branches remain. A second full run with a different particle seed base is the next checkpoint. No particle submission has been made.
